SOP: Roles & Responsibilities *

⚠️ Accountability Warning: Any deviation from this checklist—especially missing mandatory items—makes the expert fully responsible if a complaint arises.

Sales Manager

  • ✔️ Providing targets to the team.
  • ✔️ Team engagement and morale.
  • ✔️ Develop and execute sales strategies to meet company growth objectives.
  • ✔️ Coordinate with marketing, compliance, and support teams to ensure smooth service delivery.
  • ✔️ Interviewing candidates for Sales Department.
  • ✔️ Maintaining weekly, monthly, and quarterly reports for future planning.
  • ✔️ One-on-one discussions with team leads and executives to understand issues.
  • ✔️ Task assigning to team leads.
  • ✔️ Making incentive sheets.
  • ✔️ Solving queries over calls or verbally for conversion.
  • ✔️ Tracking the conversion of leads.

Sales Associate

  • ✔️Sending connections to visa-holders (F1 OPT/STEM, H1B, GC, USC) and generating leads.
  • ✔️Calling all generated leads.
  • ✔️Following up with previous and current prospects.
  • ✔️Sharing recorded calls with team lead.
  • ✔️Maintaining a personal daily leads sheet with complete information.
  • ✔️Updating a common daily leads sheet with complete information.
  • ✔️Maintaining a separate prospects/pipeline sheet.
  • ✔️Maintaining an upfront payments sheet.
  • ✔️Cross-verifying documents before onboarding.
  • ✔️Follow up with candidates who were placed previously for references.
  • ✔️Solicit Google reviews or reviews on online platforms.

Team Leads

  • ✔️Maintaining sheets: performance, payments, onboarded candidates.
  • ✔️Listening to team calls; evaluate quality and provide feedback.
  • ✔️Emailing for payment links and QC calls with relevant departments.
  • ✔️Maintaining planner for task scheduling and progress tracking.
  • ✔️Ensuring timely collection of upfront payments.
  • ✔️Solving problems for team and candidates.
  • ✔️Develop and implement strategies to generate new leads, focusing on achieving targets and sourcing specific profiles (GC, USC, H1B, Non-IT).

Assistant Manager

  • ✔️Sales Strategy and Planning.
  • ✔️Sales Team Management and Development: Helping team to achieve pre-defined targets.
  • ✔️Sales Reporting & Forecasting.
  • ✔️Lead Management.
